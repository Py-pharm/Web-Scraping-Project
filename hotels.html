About Rivers

Rivers State is an oil-rich Niger Delta State in Nigeria’s South South region. Its capital, Port Harcourt is among Nigeria’s most famous cities and among the most industrialised due to the heavy presence of oil companies in the city. An estimated 5.2 million people live in Rivers State
